Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use the logo with adequate clear space around it
  • Use provided color variants on appropriate backgrounds
  • Scale the logo proportionally
  • Use SVG format for digital applications when possible

Don't

  • Distort, stretch, or rotate the logo
  • Change the logo colors outside of provided variants
  • Add effects like shadows, gradients, or outlines
  • Place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
