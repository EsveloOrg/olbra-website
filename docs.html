Olbra Finance

Digitising Poland. Connecting Europe. Leading globally.

OLBRA Finance is establishing itself as the first exclusive issuer of a Polish zloty (PLNY) stablecoin, alongside euro (EURY) and US dollar (USDY) stablecoins. The organization aims to position Poland as a leader in digital finance through what we describe as a "triangle of stability" linking local, regional, and global monetary systems.

Key Insight

The Olbra ecosystem functions as a self-reinforcing financial loop where stablecoins provide liquidity, DeFi protocols generate yield, tokenized assets bring real-world value onchain, and payments infrastructure drives adoption.

Mission & Vision

European Financial Sovereignty

Building infrastructure that enables Europe to maintain financial autonomy in an increasingly digital world.

Three Regulatory Licenses

MiCA (EU), Genius Act (US), and EMI (Poland) compliance for full transatlantic market access.

PLNY - Polish Zloty Stablecoin

Poland's Digital Anchor

PLNY is positioned as foundational to Polish digital sovereignty. Designed for use across fintech platforms, payment service providers, and exchanges, it strengthens connections between the Polish zloty and global financial infrastructure.

1:1 PLN Backing

Every PLNY is backed by one Polish zloty in reserve

MiCA Compliant

EURY - Euro Stablecoin

Europe's Safe Haven

EURY functions as a "digital safe euro asset" with worldwide accessibility. It aims to enhance EU financial autonomy and strategic independence, serving as a compliant tokenized alternative pending an official digital euro.

Strategic Value

  • • Enables frictionless cross-border value transfer within the EU
  • • Provides digital euro exposure before official CBDC launch
  • • Bridges traditional European banking with DeFi ecosystems

USDY - US Dollar Stablecoin

The Dollar in Europe

USDY represents a geopolitical initiative - a dollar stablecoin produced in Poland. It anchors US financial interests through a trusted European ally's jurisdiction while balancing emerging euro-based alternatives and reinforcing strategic partnerships.

Global Connectivity

USDY connects the Olbra ecosystem to worldwide financial markets, enabling users to access dollar liquidity while maintaining their base in European-regulated infrastructure.
